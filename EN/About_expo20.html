Regarding our vicenarian exhibition

Compared to the beginning of term and end of term exhibitions, the vicenarian exhibition is different in how it was organised. It is launched by those who would like to participate instead of the club’s cadres. They hope to take their craft to the next level by leveraging their wealth of experiences from countless exhibitions. The works presented in the vicenarian exhibition are usually more substantial along with considerably more investments by the participants. Initially, the first group of students who held our first vicenarian exhibition happened to all be over twenty years old. That coincidence is what gave the exhibition its name..
